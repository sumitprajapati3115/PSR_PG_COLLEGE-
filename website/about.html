About PSRPGC

Purshottam Sri Ram P.G. College, Nandana-Ghatampur (KN-127) is a premier educational institution affiliated with Chhatrapati Shahu Ji Maharaj University (CSJM), Kanpur. Under the Shri Ram Group of Education, we are committed to providing quality higher education to students of Ghatampur and surrounding regions.

हमारा लक्ष्य है — "सही शिक्षा, उज्जवल भविष्य की पहचान" — हर विद्यार्थी को उच्च गुणवत्ता की शिक्षा, कुशल शिक्षकों का मार्गदर्शन, और एक उज्जवल भविष्य प्रदान करना।

Our Vision

To be a leading institution in higher education, recognized for its commitment to academic excellence, research, and the holistic development of students, empowering them to become responsible global citizens.

  • To provide quality and affordable education to all sections of society.
