Admission Eligibility

Course Eligibility Criteria
B.A. (Bachelor of Arts) 10+2 (Intermediate) in any stream from a recognized board.
B.Sc. (Bachelor of Science) 10+2 (Intermediate) with Science stream (PCM/PCB) from a recognized board.
B.Com (Bachelor of Commerce) 10+2 (Intermediate) with Commerce or Science (with Maths) stream.
M.A. (Master of Arts) Graduation in a relevant discipline from a recognized university.
M.Sc. (Master of Science) Graduation in a relevant Science discipline (e.g., B.Sc.) from a recognized university.
BCA (Bachelor of Computer Applications) 10+2 (Intermediate) with Mathematics as a subject.
B.Sc. IT (Bachelor of Science in IT) 10+2 (Intermediate) with Science stream.
B.Lib (Bachelor of Library Science) Graduation in any discipline.
B.Ed (Bachelor of Education) As per NCTE and University norms, typically requires graduation and entrance test qualification.

Note: All admissions are subject to the guidelines of CSJM University, Kanpur. Please contact the admission office for detailed information.
