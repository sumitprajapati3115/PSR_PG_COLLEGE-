A Hub of Knowledge

The Central Library of Purshottam Sri Ram P.G. College is an essential component of our outstanding research and education mission. It provides a safe, comfortable, and friendly environment that enables learning and advancement of knowledge.

  • Rich Collection: Over 10,000+ books covering various disciplines.
  • Digital Access: High-speed internet with access to e-books and online journals.
  • Reading Halls: Spacious and quiet reading rooms for focused study.
  • Magazines & Newspapers: Daily newspapers and monthly magazines to keep students updated.
