Scholarship & Fee Reimbursement

Purshottam Sri Ram P.G. College is committed to making education accessible to all. We provide complete assistance to eligible students for availing scholarship and fee reimbursement schemes offered by the Government of Uttar Pradesh.

Available Schemes:

Students belonging to the following categories can apply for the scholarship as per government norms:

  • Scheduled Caste (SC)
  • Scheduled Tribe (ST)
  • Other Backward Classes (OBC)
  • Minority Communities
  • Economically Weaker Section (EWS) - General Category
How to Apply:
  1. Students must apply online through the official UP Government Scholarship Portal: scholarship.up.gov.in
  2. After filling the online form, take a printout of the final submitted application.
  3. Submit the hard copy of the form along with all required documents (Aadhar, Income Certificate, Caste Certificate, Bank Passbook, Marksheets, etc.) at the college's scholarship department.
Please Note: The college only facilitates the process. The final approval and disbursement of the scholarship amount are at the sole discretion of the Uttar Pradesh Government.
